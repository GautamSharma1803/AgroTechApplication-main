## 🌿 Plant Disease Detection

Use AI to identify plant diseases instantly!

### How to Use:
1. Go to Home → Tap "Diagnose"
2. Choose one:
   - **Take Photo**: Use your camera
   - **Upload from Gallery**: Select an existing photo
3. Take/select a clear photo of the affected plant part
4. Tap "Analyze Plant"
5. Wait a few seconds for AI analysis

### Tips for Best Results:
- ✅ Ensure good lighting
- ✅ Focus on the affected area
- ✅ Take photos from multiple angles
- ✅ Include leaves, stems, or fruits showing symptoms
- ✅ Avoid blurry photos

### Understanding the Report:
- **Disease Name**: What's affecting your plant
- **Confidence**: How certain the AI is (higher is better)
- **Severity**: How serious it is
- **Treatment**: Step-by-step actions to take
- **Prevention**: How to avoid it in the future

### What to Do:
1. Read the recommendations carefully
2. Note the treatment steps
3. Buy recommended products from the Market
4. Monitor your plant daily
5. Consult a local expert if symptoms persist

## 🌍 Soil Health Monitoring

Track your soil's nutrients and pH levels.

### How to Test Soil:
1. Go to Home → Tap "Soil Health"
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Field Location**: e.g., "North Field"
   - **Crop Type**: What you're growing
   - **pH Level**: Test with a pH meter
   - **Nitrogen (N)**: In ppm
   - **Phosphorus (P)**: In ppm
   - **Potassium (K)**: In ppm
   - **Moisture**: Percentage (optional)
   - **Organic Matter**: Percentage (optional)
3. Tap "Generate Report"

### Getting Test Kits:
- Buy from our Marketplace
- Purchase online soil test kits
- Use professional lab testing services
- DIY kits available at garden stores

### Understanding Your Report:
- **Overall Score**: 0-100 (higher is better)
  - 80-100: Excellent
  - 60-79: Good
  - Below 60: Needs improvement
- **Parameters**: Each nutrient's status
  - Green: Optimal
  - Orange: Low
  - Blue: Normal
- **Recommendations**: What to add/change

### Acting on Results:
1. Review your score and parameters
2. Read recommendations carefully
3. Buy recommended amendments from Market
4. Retest after 30 days
